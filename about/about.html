This website belongs to CALAR.ink, a Japanese creative collective that merges art, technology, and spatial design to produce immersive, interactive experiences. Founded in 2015 during an art-tech hackathon, the group brings together artists,programmers, architects, and designers who collaborate on live painting performances and digital installations. Their work often explores the boundary between physical and virtual realities, encouraging audience participation and storytelling. Notable projects include “Achromatic World” and “Dreaming Frame,” which have been featured in events like Roppongi Art Night in Tokyo. CALAR.ink’s vision is to create poetic, emotionally engaging art that unfolds unpredictably—like ink spreading across a page—leaving a lasting impression on viewers.
